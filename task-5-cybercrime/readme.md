# Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

## Overview
This task focuses on understanding common cybercrimes and spreading awareness about how to stay safe online. As a Digital Ambassador, the goal is to educate peers about online threats and promote responsible digital behavior.

---

## Part A: Case Study
A case study was created on **UPI Payment Fraud**, one of the most common cybercrimes in India.

The case study explains:
- What UPI fraud is  
- How it typically happens step-by-step  
- Who is usually targeted  
- Possible consequences of such fraud  

This helps in understanding how easily users can be tricked if they are not aware of digital payment risks.

---

## Part B: Prevention Checklist
A “Stay Safe Online” checklist was created to provide practical tips for avoiding cybercrime.

The checklist includes:
- General online safety practices  
- Specific precautions for UPI and financial transactions  
- Awareness about suspicious links, QR codes, and fake calls  
- Official reporting channels for cybercrime  

---
